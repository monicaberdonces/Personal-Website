About me

Hi everyone! I’m Mónica Berdonces, a Neuroscience Master’s Student at the University of Helsinki in Finland. I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from Universidad Complutense de Madrid in 2026. I am fascinated by behavioral neuroscience and the deep connection between the brain and mental health.

I grew up in Talavera de la Reina, a small town close to Madrid. During my Bachelor's, I had the chance to participate in several research projects and conferences, which really sparked my interest in science. I also spent an amazing year abroad at UCLA in California. In my free time, I love reading, traveling and baking!
